Auspex 4 - Telepathy p. 138-139
Summary Use to establish a telepathic link with a target to communicate through images/concepts bidirectionally, read surface thoughts of the target, or delve deep into the target's mind to read buried memories/secrets.
Target Target must remain within sight, and must be a being with conscious thought (no constructs/normal animals).
Initial Cost 1 turn concentration; 1 Mental vs. supernaturals; Mental Challenge
Detection Telepathy usage is not visible. If the user is only eavesdropping and not projecting any thoughts, they are undetectable by the target; however, a third party using telepathy against the scanner can detect the link. The user can identify theirself telepathically before the target chooses to test/relent on the initial challenge.
Defense Target can make Willpower challenge to break off link
Projecting Thoughts
Summary The user can project thoughts to the target in the form of concepts (image, impulses, and feelings).
Extra Effect An onslaught of projected thoughts may stun or disorient a target who is unaccustomed to telepathic communication
Communication with Willing Target
Summary The user can communicate freely with a willing target, by projecting thoughts and reading the target's surface thoughts; a common language is not required.
Interrogation of Surface Thoughts
Summary The user can pluck a surface thought from the target. When used in conjunction with interrogating the target either normally or telepathically, the target's surface thoughts can be swayed to gather information. Mechanically, this works by asking a question and demanding a truthful answer.
Cost Mental Challenge per question/answer
Examples What is the appearance/name of the person/place/item about which you are speaking? What are you omitting / what is the true answer to something you lied about? What memories do you have on a specific topic of conversation?
Deep Mental Probing
Summary The user can delve deep into the target's mind to read buried memories or hidden secrets
Cost 1 Mental Trait, then a Static Mental Challenge vs. target's Mentals per question asked
Examples What is one of your flaws or negative traits? What is one of your derangements if any?
Detecting Memories Implanted by Dominate
Summary The user may be able to discern whether a memory viewed with "deep mental probing" was clumsily inserted by Dominate, by spotting poor reconstruction, gaps in the memory, etc.
Cost Additional Static Mental Challenge after "deep mental probing"was already done; available at ST's discretion only.
